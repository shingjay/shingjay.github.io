My Tools

Preamble

I believe every competent engineer should have a list of tools to simplify their lives. Here's a list of some of my favorite tools.

HomeBrew

Development

Arguably the ultimate package manager for MacOS (I say this hoping not to start a flamewar). Every developer working on a Mac should have this installed.

As of February 2021, brew 3.0 was released with support for Apple Silicon machines.

Get it here.

Spectacle App

Productivity

If you are a Windows convert just like I am, and miss the automatic readjustment of windows on your desktop, this is the tool for you! I've been using this since 2011 when I bought my first MacBook and have no complaints!

PS: It has been marked as deprecated by its creators but it still works like a charm!

Download Link

Alfred

Productivity

The perfect Spotlight replacement for Macs. If you purchase Powerpack (for just $15 for life), you can also create or use workflows to help automate your processes.

One of my favorite workflows is Caffeinate, which lets you allow/disallow your Mac from falling asleep easily.

Download Link

Things

Productivity

It's pricey for a to-do list app, but it's a very well developed app by the guys at CulturedCode.

I use this to track my work - at the end of the day I usually build up a small list of work items for the following day. The logbook feature helps me keep track of what I've done in the past; it's useful for me because I tend to forget what I need to talk about during my daily scrum session, so I just read from it out loud..
